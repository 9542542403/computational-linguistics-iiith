Word Sense Disambiguation
Introduction
Word Sense Disambiguation is a problem which governs the process of identifying sense of the word in a sentence. It uses the senses from the dictionary.


Theory

For word sense disambiguation, we need dictionary to know all the senses of words and a context in which this is needed to be disambiguated.

ex: word "bass" has two meanings: a type of fish and tones of low frequency. This information is obtained from dictionary.

Now, we will dismbiguate this word in the following sentences:
1. Fishing the bass
2. Bass line of song

Sentence 1 has first sense (fish) and Sentence 2 has second sense (tone of low frequency). This is known with the help of context words "fishing" and "song" respectively.

Not only context, but also parts of speech helps in disambiguation.


Objective

The objective of this experiment is to knoe how to do disambiguation.

Experiment
Quizzes
Give the senses for the words in bold in the following sentences.
  1. Use Google and Google it
  2. Drop the table and Water drops
  3. Water drops and drop down menu
